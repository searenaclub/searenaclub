Top Charters

Crewed and Full-Service Gulets

Gulets are traditional Mediterranean vessels crafted in Turkey, offering a unique capacity that sets them apart from sailing and motor yachts. With space for 3 to 16 cabins, gulets can comfortably accommodate groups of 6 to 32 guests, making them ideal for large groups.

Motor Yachts

For those drawn to sleek design, high speed, and premium comfort, our motor yachts offer an unmatched experience. With powerful engines and elegant interiors, they’re perfect for clients ready to invest in a high-speed, luxurious journey along the coast.

Barefoot Charters

For experienced sailors with a skipper's license, our bareboat charters offer a fully-equipped yacht without a crew. Set your course, explore hidden coves, and enjoy a personalized journey along Turkey’s stunning coast.

Premium Gulets

Our premium gulets accommodate up to 12-16 guests with ease. Designed for fuel efficiency, they offer a more economical option compared to motor yachts. Enjoy spacious interiors, elegant design, and the authentic charm of traditional Turkish sailing!

Crewed Sailing Yachts

Ideal for newcomers to sailing, our crewed yachts or catamarans provide a relaxing experience with a professional captain—and, when needed, an assistant or chef. Enjoy a thoughtfully planned itinerary, unwind, and discover the joy of sailing without any hassle.
